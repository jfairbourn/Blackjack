Welcome to Blackjack. To play, please select the "Deal" button. The object of the game is to try and reach 21 points. The face values of the cards are the number of points. Kings, Jacks, and Queens are worth 10. Ace is worth either 11 or 1, but will be handled for you. Hit "Hit" if you want another card dealt to you. Hit "Stand" if you want to stay where you are. If you exceed 21, then you have bust and will result in a tie if the dealer busts or otherwise is a loss. Have fun!
